Gory Dicing Guardians

  • itch.io

Gory Dicing Guardians (GDG) is a tower defence game where the player protects a cake against an endless horde of ants. Players can use their mouse to draw squares around ants to defeat them, but the mouse takes damage from coming into contact with ants.

GDG was a game developed for a 9-hour game jam hosted by my university's game development group. It was developed with a team in Unity.

I was responsible for:

  • Square drawing logic
  • Calculating whether ants are within the drawn square
  • Mouse logic

This involved working with a lot of math, and it was a ton of fun!
